Designer

As a Designer my goal is to simplify and plan ahead, and to shape ideas into a reality by understanding the work ahead. Each medium may be different but the process remains the same. Placing pen to paper and examining part by part, experimenting, and finding problems before they start. It’s this process, the time and energy put in that can make or break a project.
