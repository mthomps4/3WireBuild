Designer

As a Designer my goal is to simplify, plan ahead, and shape ideas into a reality by understanding the work that lies ahead. Each medium may be different but the process remains the same--placing pen to paper, examining part by part, experimenting, and finding problems before they start. The time and energy put into this process can make or break a project.
